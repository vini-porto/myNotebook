
# Excel vs Databases
### Scale
- Excel is not the most efficient tool to deal with data in large amounts. Database offer a significant advantage over spreadsheets.
### Concurrency
- In Excel only one person can edit at a time, besides databases that can handle 1000+ users working simultaneously without conflicts. ibid. [[Multi-User Access]]
### Security
- Excel can't control who sees what data. Databases can grant [[Access Control]] to specific users.
